Coercion-Resistant Vault

ERC-8300 | Step-by-step guide

What is this?

The Coercion-Resistant Vault is a smart contract wallet designed to protect cryptocurrency holders against physical coercion attacks (known as "$5 wrench attacks"). It partitions your funds into two tiers:

  • Hot balance — a small amount you can spend immediately, subject to a daily rate limit (e.g., 0.05 ETH per day).
  • Cold vault — the bulk of your funds, locked behind a timelock (e.g., 5 minutes to 72 hours) and/or requiring guardian approval to unlock.

Under coercion, you can truthfully say: "I can only send you this much right now. The rest is locked and I physically cannot access it faster." The constraint is verifiable on-chain — no deception needed.

Prerequisites

1MetaMask browser extension

Install MetaMask from metamask.io if you don't have it. It works on Chrome, Firefox, Brave, and Edge.

2Switch to Sepolia testnet

Open MetaMask, click the network dropdown (top-left), and select Sepolia test network. If you don't see it, enable "Show test networks" in MetaMask settings.

3Get Sepolia ETH

You need a small amount of Sepolia ETH (free, no real value) to interact with the vault. Get some from a faucet like sepoliafaucet.com or cloud.google.com/application/web3/faucet/ethereum/sepolia.

Connecting to the vault

4Open the demo and connect

Go to the demo page and click "Connect MetaMask". MetaMask will pop up asking you to connect. The demo automatically switches you to Sepolia if needed.

5Check your role

The banner shows whether you are the vault owner (full access) or a visitor (deposit only). This depends on whether your MetaMask address matches the vault's owner address.

Understanding the dashboard

Once connected, the dashboard shows real-time vault state:

  • Total balance — all ETH held by the vault contract.
  • Hot balance (orange) — what you can spend right now, capped by the spending limit per epoch.
  • Cold balance (blue) — locked portion. Requires timelock or guardian approval.
  • Spent this epoch — how much hot budget used in the current 24h window.
  • Unlock delay — the timelock duration (5 min in this demo).

Actions step by step

Deposit ETH

Anyone can deposit. Enter an amount, click Deposit, confirm in MetaMask. The dashboard updates after the transaction mines (~12s).

Hot spend (instant transfer)

Send ETH from the hot balance to any address, within your remaining budget.

  1. Enter recipient address and amount.
  2. Click Hot Spend and confirm in MetaMask.
  3. Transfer is instant. The "spent this epoch" counter increases.
If you exceed the hot budget, the tx reverts with "ExceedsHotBudget". Budget resets every 24h.

Cold withdrawal (timelocked)

Request a withdrawal from the cold vault. Funds are locked until the timelock expires.

  1. Enter recipient and amount, click Request Withdrawal.
  2. A pending entry appears with a countdown timer.
  3. When the countdown hits zero, click Execute to complete the transfer.
Any guardian (or the owner) can Cancel a pending withdrawal at any time before execution — this is the "panic button".

DeFi swap on Uniswap V3

The flagship demo: swap ETH for USDC through real Uniswap contracts on Sepolia.

  1. Enter an amount (e.g., 0.001 ETH), click Wrap + Swap.
  2. Confirm 3 transactions in MetaMask:
    • Tx 1: Wrap ETH into WETH via execute()
    • Tx 2: Approve router via approveToken()
    • Tx 3: Swap WETH for USDC via execute()
  3. The vault now holds USDC. Hot spending counter stays at zero.
Key insight: DeFi operations via execute() are value-preserving, so they bypass the spending limit. Only direct transfers via hotSpend() consume the budget.

Security model

Why this works under coercion

An attacker can only extract the hot balance (a small amount). The rest is timelocked. The attacker can verify this on-chain. A cold withdrawal request starts a countdown that gives you or your guardians time to cancel it.

Guardians

Trusted addresses (family, friends, custodians) who can: cancel pending withdrawals, approve instant withdrawals via multisig, pause the vault in emergencies (24h auto-expiry), and unpause with multisig.

Configuration is also protected

Spending limit increases are timelocked — an attacker cannot force you to raise the limit and drain immediately. Only security-increasing changes (lower limits) take effect instantly.

FAQ

Is this using real money?

No. Sepolia testnet only. Sepolia ETH has no real value.

"ExceedsColdBalance" error?

Your total balance is smaller than the spending limit, so all ETH is in the hot bucket. Deposit more ETH so the total exceeds the spending limit.

Uniswap swap fails?

The WETH/USDC pool on Sepolia sometimes has low liquidity. The wrap step always works and proves the mechanism.

Can I deploy my own vault?

Yes. Full source, deploy scripts, and instructions are in the GitHub repo. You need Foundry and a Sepolia RPC (free from Alchemy/Infura).
