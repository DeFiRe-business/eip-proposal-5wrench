DeFi: Swap on Uniswap V3

Execute a WETH to USDC swap via Uniswap SwapRouter02. The vault wraps ETH, approves the router, and swaps -- all while the hot spending counter stays at zero (value-preserving operation).

This runs three transactions: wrap ETH via execute(), approve router, then swap via execute().
